Internal Transfer and Purchase Receipts Backdate & Remarks Odoo App

This Odoo apps help to put custom backdate and remarks on purchase receipt and internal stock transfer. This odoo module allow receipt and internal stock transfer backdating operations in Odoo warehouse, backdate will be transfer to stock entries and accounting entries automatically when you received the goods to vendor while process incoming shipment as receipt also when you transfer the goods between internal warehouse. Whatever remarks you want to add you can add those custom remarks on purchase receipt and internal transfer, this remarks automatically pass on stock accounting journal entry if you set inventory valuation method as Automated.

Features

Add Backdate & Remarks for Internal Transfer.

User can add backdate and remarks for internal transfer.

Add Backdate & Remarks for Receipt.

User can add backdate and remarks to receipt.

Transfer Backdate in Account Entries.

Receipt and internal transfer backdate transfer to stock accounting entries.

Transfer Backdate Remarks.

Receipt and internal transfer remarks transfer to stock and product moves.

Internal Transfer

Internal Transfer Backdate & Remarks

On clicking "Validate" button new wizard will open, Here user have to select "BackDate" and "Remark" for internal transfer.

User can see backdate remarks on internal transfer.

Product Move

Under Inventory > Reporting > Product Move menu user can see created product move for internal transfer with backdate.

Stock Move

Under Inventory > Reporting > Stock Move menu user can see created stock move for internal transfer with backdate and remarks.

Stock Journal Entry

User can find backdate on created stock journal entry for internal transfer.

Product Move

Stock Move
